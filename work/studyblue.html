Site Redesign

I joined StudyBlue right at the beginning of a complete site redesign. The goal was to update the UI from a dark color scheme to a light one, and create new UX to make the site easier to use. My role began as a UX Designer writing most of the CSS and expanded into a front end engineering role as I took on more responsibilities.
